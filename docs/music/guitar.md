## Technique notes

### Bending in tune

The thing that finally helped: play the **target note** first as a fretted note,
listen, then bend up to match it. Most of my bends were landing a quarter-tone
flat. Use the [theory cheatsheet](theory.md#intervals) when I forget what a tone
vs semitone bend should sound like.

> [!TIP]
> Record the target note as a loop and bend against it. Your ears lie less when
> there's a reference ringing next to the bend.

### Vibrato

Comes from the wrist, not the finger. Anchor the thumb over the top of the neck
for leverage. Slow and wide reads as "intentional"; fast and narrow reads as
"nervous." I am usually nervous.

### String skipping

![Working through a chord shape](img/chords.jpg)

Blackbird lives or dies on clean string skips. Practice the offending bar at half
speed with the metronome, only speeding up once it's clean three times in a row.
